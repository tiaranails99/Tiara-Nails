About Us





About The Owner

Pennylane is a hardworking mother of 1. She has a total of 2 jobs, and does manicuring as her hobby. She took classes despite having to work day and night in order to learn the basics of manicuring, and she succesfully accumulated the certification for her business.

How It Started

Tiara Nails started in the year 2023 of August. She started by accepting customers and doing their nails for free in order to practice. Soon enough, as the people who she practiced with were satisfied by her service, she started the business for real.

About Tiara Nails

Tiara Nails is a home-based nail studio run by a newly certified beginner nail technician.



Please tell us about your idea and how you want it to be

Tiara Nails assure you that your desired nail design will be in your hands. We make sure that you are fully satisfied, so if one of your nail design is not into your liking, please let us know immediately so we will be able to fix it.

Having a communication is important between a client and a nail technician. You may provide us a picture of your desired design, and we will make sure to accurately copy the design you desired.



In order to avoid accidents, make sure to keep your hand still when the nail technician working on your nails.




We care about your wellbeing. Please let the nail technician know if you feel any discomfort while getting your nails done.

In order to finish the service fast, make sure not to talk with the nail technician too much to avoid distracting them, unless you wanted to comment something about their service.
